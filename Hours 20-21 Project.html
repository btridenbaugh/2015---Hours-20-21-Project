TOP 5 HEROES


Green Lantern - Green Lantern is the name of a group of superheroes that fight evil with the aid of rings that grant them powers. Hal Jordan, who is one of the Green Lanterns, is one of the co-founders of the Justice League.

Captain America - Captain America is a man with superhuman strength. He is typically found with his shield, and was previously a soldier. He is also the leader of the Avengers.

Flash - Flash is a superhuman who has the abilities of superspeed, as well as super reflexes. Another ability is that they can effect the laws of physics, as well as travel time. He is also one of the co-founders of the Justice League.

Iron Man - Iron Man is a millionaire engineer, that created a suit thta is able to contol the energy of an arc reactor of his own design. He is also one of the co-founders of the Avengers.

Batman - Batman is a millionaire, and after his parents were killed, he trained himself to face evil. He is also one of the co-founders of the Justice League.
